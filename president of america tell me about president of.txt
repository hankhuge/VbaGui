The President of the United States POTUS  POHts is the head of state and head of government of the United States of America. The president directs the executive branch of the federal government and is the commanderinchief of the United States Armed Forces.
In contemporary times, the president is looked upon as the worlds most powerful political figure and as the leader of the only current global superpower. The role includes responsibility for the worlds most expensive military that has the second largest nuclear arsenal. The president also leads the nation with the largest economy by nominal GDP. The president possesses significant domestic and international hard and soft power.
Article II of the Constitution establishes the executive branch of the federal government.
