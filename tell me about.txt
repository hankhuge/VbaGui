Jabalpur formerly Jubbulpore is a tier 2 city in the state of Madhya Pradesh, India. It is one of the most famous cities of Madhya Pradesh. According to the 2011 census, it is the thirdlargest urban agglomeration in Madhya Pradesh, and the countrys 30thlargest urban agglomeration.
The city is large and growing it is a major education center in India. The High Court of MP is in Jabalpur and so are many Government administrative headquarters. It is one of the major centers for the production of arms and ammunition and military base in India.
